# ETSystemsAI

**ETSystemsAI** is an applied AI engineering lab focused on building
**reliable, auditable, and production-ready AI systems**.

We specialize in the infrastructure *around* AI models:
evaluation, observability, deterministic execution, and backend-first AI architectures.

## Core Focus Areas

- **AI Systems Reliability & Evaluation**
  - LLM failure taxonomy
  - Reproducible evaluation pipelines
  - Stress-testing and fallback design

- **Trustworthy & Auditable AI**
  - Deterministic reasoning flows
  - Structured logging and traceability
  - Compliance-aware AI system design

- **Production AI Infrastructure**
  - Backend-first architectures
  - Distributed AI services
  - Multi-tenant AI platforms

- **Domain-Grounded AI Systems**
  - Regulatory and legal RAG pipelines
  - Continuously updated knowledge bases
  - Human-in-the-loop validation

## Active Projects

- **WineDo** — AI automation platform for wine tourism and CRM
- **Regulatory RAG & Evaluation Framework** — reliability tooling for LLM-based systems in regulated domains
- **AI Support Automation (Accounting domain)** — LLM-powered customer support grounded in tax and regulatory data

## Philosophy

We do not build demos.

We build **systems that survive real users, real data, and real failure modes**.
